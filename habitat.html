Animal Habitat Info

Otters can be found in several regions of the planet, including large areas of North and South America, and Europe. They live in or around water (depending on specie) and will sleep on land, except for saltwater otters, which will sleep in strands of kelp floating on the surface of the water. The kelp is used to keep the otters from floating away while they sleep. These land-based otters are not particular about where they sleep, having dens in or around areas of moderate disturbance. These otters commonly have several sleeping dens.
